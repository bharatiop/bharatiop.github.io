Focus Areas

What we study

⭐

Equation of State

Deciphering the composition of dense matter inside neutron stars — what they are made of and how matter behaves at nuclear densities.

🔮

Hyperons in Neutron Stars

Exploring strangeness-bearing particles (hyperons) at the core of massive neutron stars and their effect on stellar properties.

🌡

Thermal Evolution

Investigating how the internal temperature of isolated and accreting neutron stars evolves over cosmic timescales.

🌑

Dark Matter Constraints

Using neutron star observations to constrain dark matter — uncovering potential signatures and interactions in these cosmic laboratories.
