    <h3 style="font-size:17px; font-weight:700; color:#0b1a2e; margin-bottom:12px;">What we work on</h3>
    <p style="font-size:14.5px; color:#5a6275; line-height:1.8;">
      NAP Lab studies the physics of neutron stars using relativistic mean-field models, connecting nuclear theory with 
      multi-messenger observations (LIGO/Virgo, NICER, radio pulsars). Current research topics include: 
      <strong>equation of state of dense matter</strong>, <strong>dark matter in neutron stars</strong>, 
      <strong>quasinormal modes &amp; gravitational waves</strong>, <strong>hyperon physics</strong>, and 
      <strong>thermal evolution</strong> of neutron stars.
    </p>
