    <h2 class="section-title">What you could work on</h2>
  </div>

  <div style="display:grid;grid-template-columns:repeat(auto-fill,minmax(280px,1fr));gap:14px;margin-bottom:52px;">
    <div style="border:0.5px solid var(--border);border-radius:var(--radius-sm);padding:18px 20px;">
      <span class="tag" style="margin-bottom:10px;display:inline-block;">NS Asteroseismology</span>
      <p style="font-size:13px;color:var(--text-muted);line-height:1.7;">Radial and non-radial oscillation modes (f/p/w-modes) in full GR, universal relations, proto-neutron star oscillations.</p>
    </div>
    <div style="border:0.5px solid var(--border);border-radius:var(--radius-sm);padding:18px 20px;">
      <span class="tag" style="margin-bottom:10px;display:inline-block;">Dark Matter in NSs</span>
      <p style="font-size:13px;color:var(--text-muted);line-height:1.7;">WIMP/fermionic dark matter admixed neutron stars; multi-messenger constraints; PSR J0952-0607 and HESS J1731-347.</p>
    </div>
    <div style="border:0.5px solid var(--border);border-radius:var(--radius-sm);padding:18px 20px;">
      <span class="tag" style="margin-bottom:10px;display:inline-block;">Multi-Messenger &amp; GW</span>
      <p style="font-size:13px;color:var(--text-muted);line-height:1.7;">GW170817 parameter estimation, NICER X-ray constraints, EoS Bayesian inference with LIGO/NICER/radio data.</p>
    </div>
    <div style="border:0.5px solid var(--border);border-radius:var(--radius-sm);padding:18px 20px;">
      <span class="tag" style="margin-bottom:10px;display:inline-block;">Nuclear EoS</span>
      <p style="font-size:13px;color:var(--text-muted);line-height:1.7;">Relativistic mean-field models; hyperons; delta-baryons; quarkyonic matter; twin-star configurations from phase transitions.</p>
    </div>
    <div style="border:0.5px solid var(--border);border-radius:var(--radius-sm);padding:18px 20px;">
      <span class="tag" style="margin-bottom:10px;display:inline-block;">Thermal Evolution</span>
      <p style="font-size:13px;color:var(--text-muted);line-height:1.7;">Proto-neutron star cooling, finite-temperature EoS, Urca processes, superfluidity and pairing gaps in dense matter.</p>
    </div>
    <div style="border:0.5px solid var(--border);border-radius:var(--radius-sm);padding:18px 20px;">
      <span class="tag" style="margin-bottom:10px;display:inline-block;">Computational Tools</span>
      <p style="font-size:13px;color:var(--text-muted);line-height:1.7;">Contributing to open-source TOV solvers, EoS table generation, oscillation mode codes, and compact-common infrastructure.</p>
    </div>
