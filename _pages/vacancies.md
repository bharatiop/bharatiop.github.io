    <h3 style="font-size:16px;font-weight:600;color:var(--accent);margin-bottom:10px;">What we work on</h3>
    <p style="font-size:14px;color:var(--text-muted);line-height:1.8;">
      NAP Lab studies the physics of neutron stars using relativistic mean-field models, connecting nuclear theory with multi-messenger observations (LIGO/Virgo, NICER, radio pulsars). Current research topics include: <strong>equation of state of dense matter</strong>, <strong>dark matter in neutron stars</strong>, <strong>quasinormal modes &amp; gravitational waves</strong>, <strong>hyperon physics</strong>, and <strong>thermal evolution</strong> of neutron stars.
    </p>
