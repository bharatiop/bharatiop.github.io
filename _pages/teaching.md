    <h2 class="section-title">Courses at NIT Rourkela</h2>
  </div>

  <div class="course-list" style="margin-bottom:52px;">

    <div class="course-item">
      <span class="course-code">PH 5202</span>
      <div>
        <h4>Nuclear Physics</h4>
        <p>Postgraduate core course for M.Sc. Physics. Covers nuclear structure, nuclear forces, radioactive decay, nuclear reactions, fission/fusion, and introductory nuclear astrophysics (nucleosynthesis, neutron stars).</p>
        <span class="course-sem">Semester: Even · M.Sc. Physics, NIT Rourkela</span>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="course-item">
      <span class="course-code">PH 3101</span>
      <div>
        <h4>Quantum Mechanics</h4>
        <p>Core undergraduate/postgraduate course. Topics include the Schrödinger equation, operators and observables, hydrogen atom, angular momentum, perturbation theory, and introduction to scattering theory.</p>
        <span class="course-sem">Semester: Odd · B.Tech. / M.Sc. Physics, NIT Rourkela</span>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="course-item">
      <span class="course-code">PH 2102</span>
      <div>
        <h4>Waves and Optics</h4>
        <p>Undergraduate engineering physics course on wave mechanics, interference, diffraction, polarisation, and optical instruments. Intended for B.Tech. students across all disciplines.</p>
        <span class="course-sem">Semester: Even · B.Tech. (all branches), NIT Rourkela</span>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="course-item">
      <span class="course-code">PH 5301</span>
      <div>
        <h4>Astrophysics</h4>
        <p>Postgraduate elective covering stellar structure and evolution, compact objects (white dwarfs, neutron stars, black holes), cosmology fundamentals, and multi-messenger astronomy including gravitational waves.</p>
        <span class="course-sem">Semester: Odd (elective) · M.Sc. Physics, NIT Rourkela</span>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="course-item">
      <span class="course-code">PH 4014</span>
      <div>
        <h4>Atomic &amp; Molecular Physics</h4>
        <p>Upper-undergraduate course on atomic structure, fine and hyperfine structure, multi-electron atoms, molecular bonding, spectroscopy, and lasers. Builds on quantum mechanics and electromagnetism.</p>
        <span class="course-sem">Semester: Even · B.Tech. Physics Minor / M.Sc., NIT Rourkela</span>
      </div>
    </div>

  </div>
